Freezer Cookie Instructions

Chewy or crunchy—your call.

Slice the cookie dough (straight from the freezer) into 2 cm slices (should give you 6 or 7 cookies). Place cookies onto the greaseproof paper the dough comes wrapped in and bake at 180 °C for approximately 10 mins or until a light golden brown if you like them chewy. Cook until mid golden brown for crunchy and enjoy!!

These cookies contain

  • Wheat flour
  • Sugar
  • Egg
  • Vanilla
  • Milk chocolate
  • Baking powder
Tip: Ovens vary—check at 8 minutes, then watch the color. Pull earlier for chewy, later for crunchy.
Baked from frozen. No thawing needed.
